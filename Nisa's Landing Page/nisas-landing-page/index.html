Nisa’s was created in 1999 using our family’s longstanding recipe, handed down from generation to generation, to provide the general public with an authentic and flavoursome experience. This allowed consumers an option to spend less time cooking and more time with their family whilst still enjoying the homemade quality food we all love.

Ever since the birth of Nisa’s, we pride ourselves on producing the highest quality products whilst using freshly sourced vegetables and premium quality Halal meat.

One by one, we have produced hundreds of millions of our samosas and spring rolls by hand, maintaining the premium quality and authentic taste. Straying away from the hand making process will be a cheaper process for us but we will not do anything to compromise on our quality and taste.

Samosas

All meats for our products are minced on-site with the finest quality ingredients.

Our meat is pre-cooked and drained of excess fat to provide an exceptional taste.

Spring Rolls

We take pride in only using the highest quality ingredients for our products.

Vegetables are pre-cooked individually and then combined to ensure each component of our product is cooked to perfection.

Seekh Kebabs

Our Seekh Kebabs are pre-cooked on authentic open flame grill and then frozen right away to lock in the beautiful taste, maintain freshness and provide convenience to our customers.

We handcraft each and every Seekh Kebab to ensure a soft and fluffy finish. Each Kebab is truly unique.

Shami Kebabs

We slow cook our Shami Kebabs for several hours to create an incredible soft texture.

The meat is drained of excess fat to provide an exceptional taste. Our process ensures a truly unique product which is ready to eat within minutes.
